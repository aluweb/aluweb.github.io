    MathJax vs. KaTeX

    There are basically two options for rending math. MathJax is the default option for Nikola and also for Jupyter notebooks.

    On the other side, KaTeX seems to be much faster to render, and there is no obvious re-rendering after page reloading. The font quality is also nice or maybe even better than MathJax. But I have faced serious problems that made me give up using KaTeX for my site. For example, I wasn't able to insert inline math using standard $..$ directive, and TeX environments produced by Nikola are also not supported. All this makes KaTeX still too immature for usage with Nikola and Jupyter.
